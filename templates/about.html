Our Story

A journey of passion, flavor, and tradition

Est. 2010

A Passion Born
in the Kitchen

Savoria was born from a simple dream: to create a place where food tells a story. Founded by Chef Marco Russo and his partner Elena in 2010, what started as a small 30-seat bistro has grown into one of the city's most celebrated dining destinations.

Marco spent 15 years training under legendary chefs in Lyon, Milan, and Tokyo before returning home with a vision to blend classical techniques with modern innovation. Every dish on our menu carries a piece of that journey.

Today, Savoria holds 2 Michelin stars and has been voted "Best Restaurant in the City" four years running — but what matters most to us is seeing the joy on every guest's face.

Chef Marco RussoFounder & Executive Chef

Our Journey

The Savoria Timeline

2010

The Beginning

Marco and Elena open Savoria as a small 30-seat bistro in downtown Manhattan.

2013

First Michelin Star

Savoria earns its first Michelin star, establishing itself as a destination restaurant.

2016

Expansion

The restaurant expands to 120 seats and introduces the iconic open kitchen concept.

2019

Second Michelin Star

After years of relentless pursuit of perfection, Savoria receives its second Michelin star.

2022

Sustainability Pledge

Savoria achieves carbon-neutral status and partners exclusively with regenerative farms.

2026

Today

Over 50,000 guests served, 12 awards, and still as passionate as day one.
